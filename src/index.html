Restaurant

Welcome to Abu, a culinary journey into the heart of the Middle East, right here in Prague. We take pride in serving a diverse menu inspired by the rich traditions of Lebanese, Turkish, and Iraqi cuisines. Our dishes are prepared using authentic recipes passed down through generations, blending aromatic spices and fresh ingredients to create an unforgettable dining experience.


At Abu, we aim to transport you to a world of warm hospitality, vibrant flavors, and timeless traditions. Whether you’re joining us for a casual meal, a family gathering, or a special celebration, we promise a truly unique and flavorful experience.

The bar

Welcome to Abu Bar, where the spirit of the Middle East meets the vibrant nightlife of Prague. Our bar offers an exquisite selection of handcrafted cocktails, premium wines, and traditional Middle Eastern beverages, all carefully curated to complement our cuisine. Enjoy classic favorites like Arak, Turkish Raki, or our signature cocktails infused with exotic spices and flavors.


With a cozy, stylish ambiance, Abu Bar is the perfect place to unwind, socialize, or start your evening. Whether you’re enjoying a drink before dinner, gathering with friends, or looking for a unique night out, we offer an unforgettable experience with every sip.
